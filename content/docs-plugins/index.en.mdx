## Download Plugin

Visit the official iPaymu website at [https://ipaymu.com/](https://ipaymu.com/), then select **Doc API -> Download Instant Plugin**.

![Download Plugin](/img/plugins/image1.jpeg)

Choose your iPaymu plugin according to what you need. At iPaymu, we provide plugins for **WP WooCommerce**, **OpenCart 3.x**, **Prestashop 1.x**, **WHMCS**, and **Moodle**.

![Choose Plugin](/img/plugins/image2.jpeg)

## WP WooCommerce

Follow the steps below to install the WP WooCommerce plugin:

1. **Login** to your WordPress dashboard, then select the **Plugins** menu -> **Add New**.

![Add New Plugin](/img/plugins/image3.jpeg)

2. Click **Upload Plugin**, and upload the plugin you downloaded in `.zip` format.

![Upload Plugin](/img/plugins/image4.jpeg)
![Upload Zip](/img/plugins/image5.jpeg)

3. If the plugin is successfully installed, it will be visible in the **Installed Plugins** menu, then activate the plugin.

![Activate Plugin](/img/plugins/image6.jpeg)

4. Then, choose **WooCommerce Menu -> Settings**.

![WooCommerce Settings](/img/plugins/image7.jpeg)

5. Select the **Payments** menu tab, activate the **iPaymu Payment Gateway** payment method, and click the **manage** button to configure the plugin.

![Manage Payment](/img/plugins/image8.jpeg)

6. Fill in the form that appears according to your store data. For your **API Key**, it can be seen on your iPaymu dashboard.

![Configure Plugin](/img/plugins/image9.jpeg)

7. If your shop is in sandbox mode, tick the **Mode Sandbox/Development** checkbox.

![Sandbox Mode](/img/plugins/image10.jpeg)

8. If you want to custom your **“Thank You”** page (the page that appears after checkout), all you have to do is fill in the page URL link here.

![Thank You Page](/img/plugins/image11.jpeg)

9. When finished, click **Save Changes**.

## Opencart

### Method 1

1. **Login** to your Opencart dashboard page.

![Login Opencart](/img/plugins/image15.jpeg)

2. After logging in, click the **Extensions** menu -> **Installer** then click the **Upload** button.

![Opencart Installer](/img/plugins/image16.jpeg)

3. Upload your downloaded plugin in `.zip` format.

![Upload Opencart](/img/plugins/image17.jpeg)

4. A success message will appear below if the plugin is successfully installed.

![Success Opencart](/img/plugins/image18.png)

5. Then click **Extensions -> Extensions**, select filter **Payments**. Find **iPaymu**, then click **Install** (green icon).

![Install iPaymu](/img/plugins/image19.jpeg)

6. After installing, click the **Edit** button (blue icon).

![Edit iPaymu](/img/plugins/image20.jpeg)

7. Fill the form with your username and Api Key on your iPaymu account dashboard. Then click **Save**.

![Configure Opencart](/img/plugins/image21.jpeg)

## Prestashop

1. **Login** to your PrestaShop dashboard page.

![Login Prestashop](/img/plugins/image22.jpeg)

2. Click the **Modules** menu -> **Module Manager** then click the **Upload a Module** button.

![Module Manager](/img/plugins/image23.jpeg)

3. Upload your downloaded plugin in `.zip` format.

![Upload Prestashop](/img/plugins/image24.jpeg)

4. If successfully installed, a success message will appear. Click **Configure**.

5. Fill the form with your username and Api Key on your iPaymu account dashboard. Then click **Save**.

## WHMCS

**WHMCS** (Web Host Manager Complete Solution) is a hosting business automation platform widely used by Hosting Providers, Domain Registrars, Web Agencies, and Developers.

1. Download the WHMCS plugin from the [iPaymu Plugin page](https://ipaymu.com/id/plugin-download/).

![Download WHMCS Plugin](/img/plugins/whmcs/image1.webp)

2. After extracting, copy the `callback/` folder to `/modules/gateways/callback/` and the `ipaymu/` folder and `ipaymu.php` file to `/modules/gateways/`.

3. Go to **Settings** → **Apps & Integrations**, select **Browse** → **Payments**.

![Settings Apps & Integrations](/img/plugins/whmcs/image2.webp)

![Browse Payments](/img/plugins/whmcs/image3.webp)

4. Find **iPaymu Direct Payment**, click **Activate**. Once active, click **Manage**.

![Activate iPaymu Direct Payment](/img/plugins/whmcs/image4.webp)

![Module Active Status](/img/plugins/whmcs/image5.webp)

5. Enter **Environment** (`Sandbox` or `Production`), **VA Number**, and **API Key**. Click **Save Changes**.

![iPaymu Environment Settings](/img/plugins/whmcs/image6.webp)

6. Set up **Product Group**, **Product**, and **Pricing** as needed.

![Product Group and Product](/img/plugins/whmcs/image8.webp)

7. Customer selects a product → **Order Now** → **Checkout** → choose **iPaymu Direct Payment** → **Complete Order**.

![Payment Method Selection](/img/plugins/whmcs/image12.webp)

8. Customer is redirected to the iPaymu payment page. After successful payment, the invoice status changes to **PAID**.

![Invoice Status PAID](/img/plugins/whmcs/image16.webp)

For the complete guide, visit the [WHMCS Plugin](/en/docs-plugins/whmcs) page.

## Moodle

The **Moodle Plugin** is an add-on component for the Moodle e-learning platform that enables course payment integration via iPaymu.

1. Download the Moodle plugin from the [iPaymu Plugin page](https://ipaymu.com/id/plugin-download/).

2. Log in as **Admin** to your Moodle site, go to **Site administration** → **Plugins** → **Install plugins**.

3. Upload the plugin `.zip` file, click **Install plugin from the ZIP file**, then click **Continue**.

4. Click **Upgrade Moodle database now**. Once finished, set the **Environment** (`Sandbox` or `Production`), enter your **API Key** and **VA Number**.

5. Enable the **iPaymu Payment Enrolment Plugin**. Make sure the plugin is active with the **eye** icon glowing blue.

6. Create a new course via **New Course**, fill in the description, then click **Save and display**.

7. Click the **Participants** tab → **Enrolment methods** → **Add method** → select **iPaymu Payment**.

8. Fill in the description and course price, then click **Add method**.

9. When a user accesses the course, a payment page will appear. Click **Pay via iPaymu**.

10. After successful payment, the user can immediately access the course content.

For the complete guide, visit the [Moodle Plugin](/en/docs-plugins/moodle) page.
