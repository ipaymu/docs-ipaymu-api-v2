---
title: FAQ Guide for Merchant Websites
description: Minimum FAQ content guide that must be available on your merchant website before submitting iPaymu verification.
---

One of the requirements for iPaymu merchant website verification is having an informative and publicly accessible **FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)** page. This page helps the verification team understand your business and assess integration eligibility.

<Callout type="warn" title="Required Before Verification">
Ensure your FAQ page is **live and publicly accessible** before submitting for verification. A missing or incomplete FAQ may cause your verification to be rejected or delayed.
</Callout>

Below are the minimum questions that **must** be answered in your website's FAQ:

---

## 1. What services does this company provide?

Briefly explain:
- Your company's line of business
- Products or services offered
- Key benefits provided to customers

**Example:**
> "We provide professional graphic design services for small and medium business branding, including logo creation, digital banners, and printed promotional materials."

---

## 2. Are the services or products offered legal?

Explain:
- Legal status of your business (registered, licensed, or in process)
- Business license number or relevant certifications (if any)
- Compliance with applicable regulations in Indonesia

**Example:**
> "Our business is legally registered in Indonesia with a NIB (Business Identification Number) and operates in accordance with applicable laws and regulations."

---

## 3. How do customers place an order or use the service?

Explain the steps customers must follow to:
- Purchase products or use services
- Complete checkout and payment
- Receive order confirmation

**Example:**
> "Select the desired service → Fill in the order form → Complete payment using an available method → Receive order confirmation via email."

---

## 4. How does the company protect customer data?

Explain:
- Your company's commitment to customer data security
- What data is collected and how it is used
- The existence of a Privacy Policy (and link to that page if available)

**Example:**
> "Customer personal data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties without consent. Read our full [Privacy Policy](#)."

---

## 5. How can customers contact the company?

List all official communication channels, including:
- An actively monitored email address
- WhatsApp or phone number (if available)
- Official social media accounts (if available)
- Customer service operating hours

**Example:**
> "Contact us via email: store@example.com or WhatsApp: 0812-xxxx-xxxx. We are available Monday–Saturday, 09:00–17:00 WIB."

---

<Callout type="tip" title="Tips for Passing Verification">
- FAQ answers must be **specific to your business** — avoid overly generic answers or copy-paste templates without customization.
- Ensure all listed contact information is **active and reachable**.
- A well-written FAQ demonstrates business credibility to the iPaymu verification team.
</Callout>
