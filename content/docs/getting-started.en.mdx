Welcome to iPaymu! Follow these steps to start integrating iPaymu payment gateway into your application.

<Steps>
<Step>

## Registration

First, you need to create an account on iPaymu.

![Registration Flow](/img/getting-started/register.png)

1.  Visit [iPaymu Registration Page](https://my.ipaymu.com/register).
2.  Fill in your personal and business details.
3.  Verify your email address.

</Step>

<Step>

## Login

Once registered, log in to your iPaymu dashboard.

![Login Flow](/img/getting-started/login.png)

1.  Go to [iPaymu Login Page](https://my.ipaymu.com/login).
2.  Enter your email and password.

</Step>

<Step>

## Integration Settings

To start using the API, you need to retrieve your API Key and Virtual Account (VA) number.

![Integration Page](/img/getting-started/integration.png)

1.  In the dashboard sidebar, navigate to **Integration**.
2.  Here you will find your **VA Number** and **API Key**.
3.  **Important**: Ensure you are in the correct mode (Sandbox for testing, Production for live transactions). The keys are different for each mode.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Sandbox Mode">
  Use **Sandbox Mode** for development and testing. You can switch modes in the top right corner of
  the dashboard.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warn" title="Production Requirements">
  Before going live, ensure your server uses a **Static IP** and your domain is registered. Read
  more in the [IP & Domain Validation](./ip-domain-validation) guide.
</Callout>
