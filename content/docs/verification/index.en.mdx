To activate your production account, you need to complete the verification process.

## Requirements

- KTP (ID Card)
- NPWP (Tax ID)
- Valid Bank Account

## Steps

1. Login to your iPaymu Dashboard.
2. Go to **Account** > **Verification**.
3. Upload the required documents.
4. Wait for approval (usually 1x24 hours).

<Callout type="info">Ensure all documents are clear and readable to avoid rejection.</Callout>
